About Us

What is SANDING?

SANDING or Sansan Coding is a community dedicated to coding enthusiasts, both beginners and professionals, who aim to enhance their skills in programming. The community serves as a platform for learning, sharing knowledge, and collaborating on coding projects.

Our mission

  • Create a supportive environment where members can collaborate on coding projects, share knowledge, and help each other solve problems.
  • Host hackathons, coding challenges, and open-source projects to encourage collaboration and innovation.
  • Organize webinars, workshops, and mentoring sessions with industry experts.
